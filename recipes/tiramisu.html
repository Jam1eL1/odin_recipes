Tiramisu

What is Tiramisu?

Tiramisu is an Italian dessert

Ingredients

  • Ladyfinger biscuits (Savoiardi)
  • Espresso coffee, cooled
  • Mascarpone cheese
  • Eggs (separated)
  • Sugar
  • Cocoa powder

Steps

  1. Prepare Espresso: Brew espresso coffee and let it cool.
  2. Prepare Cream Mixture: Beat egg yolks with sugar, then add mascarpone cheese until smooth.
  3. Whip Egg Whites: In a separate bowl, whip egg whites until stiff peaks form.
  4. Combine Mixtures: Gently fold whipped egg whites into the mascarpone mixture.
  5. Layer Tiramisu: Dip ladyfingers into espresso, layer them with the cream mixture in a dish.
  6. Chill: Refrigerate for several hours or overnight.
  7. Finish: Before serving, dust with cocoa powder.
