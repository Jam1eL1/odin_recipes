Gnocchi

What is Gnocchi?

What Is Gnocchi? Gnocchi (singular gnocco) are Italian dumplings made with flour, eggs, and potatoes. Gnocchi can be served in tomato sauce, tossed with pesto, or sautéed with butter and herbs.

Ingredients

  • 3 medium russet potatoes, scrubbed
  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour, plus more for dusting
  • 1 large egg

Steps

  1. Boil Potatoes: Cook potatoes until tender.
  2. Mash Potatoes: Peel and mash potatoes.
  3. Mix Ingredients: Combine mashed potatoes, flour, egg, and salt.
  4. Form Dough: Knead into a dough.
  5. Shape Gnocchi: Roll dough into ropes, cut into pieces, and indent with a fork.
  6. Cook Gnocchi: Boil in salted water until they float.
  7. Serve: Toss with sauce and enjoy!
